About Me

Hello, I’m James Dafeamekpor, a passionate Computer Engineering student with a solid foundation in cloud computing, system administration, and database management. My tech journey is fueled by a genuine curiosity to understand how things work—and more importantly, how they can solve real-world problems.

During high school, I collaborated with global partners from Graz University, Austria and MIT on mobile learning initiatives, igniting my passion for innovation and technology. Later, at GRO Oilfield Alliances, I gained hands-on experience in the energy sector—working with lifting equipment engineering, which sharpened my technical problem-solving skills and deepened my commitment to tech.

Over the years, I’ve explored cloud platforms like AWS, deploying and managing EC2 instances, integrating with S3 buckets, and developing Python-based applications. I also worked with fellow university colleagues to build a full-featured school management system using Python and Django.

I’m currently focused on expanding my portfolio, showcasing projects in system administration, web development, and backend integration. I’m always open to new opportunities, collaborations, and challenges that inspire innovation and growth.

Feel free to explore my work and get in touch if you'd like to connect or collaborate!
